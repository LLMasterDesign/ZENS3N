**Best for:** BTC/ETH when trending up or down

**Setup:**

### Strategy 2: Support/Resistance Bounce
**Best for:** Ranging markets (sideways movement)

**Setup:**
- 1-hour chart
- Identify clear support/resistance levels
- Need 2+ touches of each level

**Long Entry (Buy):**
1. Price approaches support level
2. RSI below 35 (oversold)
3. Volume spike
4. Bullish reversal candle (hammer, engulfing)
5. Buy at close of reversal candle

**Exit:**
- Target: Resistance level OR 5% gain (whichever comes first)
- Stop: Just below support (-1.5%)

**Example:**
```
ETH trading between $3,000 (support) and $3,200 (resistance)
Price drops to $3,010
RSI: 32 (oversold)
Bullish hammer candle forms
Entry: $3,015
Stop: $2,970 (-1.5%)
Target: $3,165 (resistance, +5%)
```

---

### Strategy 3: Breakout Trading (Advanced)
**Best for:** After 90 days of experience

**Setup:**
- 4-hour chart
- Price consolidating in tight range
- Decreasing volume (coiling up)

**Long Entry (Buy):**
1. Price breaks ABOVE resistance with volume spike
2. Volume 2x average
3. RSI above 60 but below 80
4. Enter on breakout OR wait for retest of broken resistance

**Exit:**
- Target: Measured move (height of consolidation added to breakout)
- Stop: Below breakout level (-3%)

**Only use this after mastering Strategies 1 & 2!**

## ğŸš¨ COMMON MISTAKES & HOW TO AVOID THEM

### Mistake #1: Trading Too Much
**Problem:** Over-trading leads to fees eating profits
**Solution:** Max 3 trades per day, quality > quantity

### Mistake #2: No Stop Loss
**Problem:** One bad trade wipes out weeks of gains
**Solution:** ALWAYS set stop loss before entering

### Mistake #3: Revenge Trading
**Problem:** Trying to "win back" losses leads to bigger losses
**Solution:** 3-loss rule (stop after 3 losses)

### Mistake #4: FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out)
**Problem:** Buying pumps at the top
**Solution:** Only trade your setups, ignore noise

### Mistake #5: No Plan
**Problem:** Random trades = gambling
**Solution:** Write your plan, follow it religiously

### Mistake #6: Ignoring Fees
**Problem:** Fees can turn wins into losses
**Solution:** Use limit orders, choose low-fee exchanges

### Mistake #7: Not Journaling
**Problem:** Can't learn from mistakes
**Solution:** Journal EVERY trade (template above)
