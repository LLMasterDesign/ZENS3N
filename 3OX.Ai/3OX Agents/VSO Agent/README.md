**Your AI-powered Veterans Service Officer for VA disability claims.**

Navigating VA disability claims doesn't have to be overwhelming. VSO.AGENT is a specialized AI agent that works like a trained Veterans Service Officer that is tasked with asking the right questions, organizing your evidence, tracking deadlines, and guiding you through every step of the claims process.

### What Makes VSO.AGENT Different

**Strategic Questioning**: Uses proven methodology to systematically assess your claim situation. Not just answers. The right questions that uncover what matters.

**Evidence Organization**: Maintains a structured filing system for medical records, service documentation, and supporting evidence. Everything in its place, nothing lost in the shuffle.

**DBQ Expertise**: Instant access to Disability Benefits Questionnaire requirements for every condition. Know exactly what the VA needs before your exam.

**VA Regulations at Your Fingertips**: References 38 CFR and M21-1 on demand. Understand the rules that govern your claim, in plain language.

**Deadline Guardian**: Tracks every important date: appeals windows, exam schedules, evidence submissions. Never miss a critical deadline again.

**Current Policy Search**: Pulls the latest VA policies and procedures. Stay ahead of changes that affect your claim.

**Built for Veterans, By Design**: Operates with military precision. Atomic operations, checksum validation, systematic backups. Your claim data deserves the same reliability you provided in service.

## Why VSO.AGENT?

The VA claims process is complex by design. Forms, deadlines, medical evidence, legal language. It's a battlefield where veterans often fight alone. VSO.AGENT changes that.

**Know What to Ask**: Most veterans don't know what questions to answer until it's too late. VSO.AGENT guides you through strategic questioning that surfaces critical details for your claim.

**Stay Organized**: Scattered medical records and lost documents sink claims. VSO.AGENT maintains military-grade organization for every piece of evidence.

**Understand the System**: VA regulations aren't written for clarity. VSO.AGENT translates complex rules into actionable guidance.

**Never Fly Blind**: Track your claim status, understand next steps, and anticipate what's coming. Knowledge is power in the claims process.

## The Technology

VSO.AGENT runs on the 3OX framework: a kernel-style architecture that brings operating system reliability to AI agents.

- **Ruby runtime** for flexibility and clarity
- **Rust brain** for compiled performance and type safety
- **vec3 kernel** with protected surfaces (rc, lib, dev, var)
- **Immutable rules** that enforce best practices
- **Receipt-based operations** that prove every action

No black boxes. No lost context. Every operation logged, verified, and traceable.
